About Us

Trader is about promoting free use and freedom of speech. Everyone should have the chance to play games they love and adore.

Our company is dedicated to the Development and distribution of games through our service Trader which allows you to do missions to earn our currency "Y Coins", then trade them in for our products. Our Products consists of games and software which are all made in house.

We are based in Australia, Our Founder is Jayden Anderson with our funding coming out of his pocket.
